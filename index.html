Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you book with confidence.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all! Many of our clients provide us with a spare key, a door code, or lockbox access so we can clean while they are at work or out of the house. Your home is completely safe with our vetted and insured professionals.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes, we provide all high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning supplies and professional-grade equipment (including vacuums and mops). If you have a specific product you'd prefer us to use, just let us know!

Are your cleaners insured?

Absolutely. QB2 Active Cleaning is fully insured. This means that in the highly unlikely event that something is damaged during a clean, you are completely protected.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that life happens. We kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your cleaning appointment to avoid a cancellation fee.
